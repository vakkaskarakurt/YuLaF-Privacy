## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

### Does YuLaF collect my browsing history?
**No.** YuLaF only reads video titles and channel names on YouTube to detect language. It does not track or store your browsing history.

### Is my data sent to external servers?
**No.** All language detection happens locally on your device using Chrome's built-in APIs. No data is transmitted to external servers.

### What permissions does YuLaF need and why?

| Permission | Why We Need It |
|------------|----------------|
| `activeTab` | To read video titles on YouTube for language detection |
| `storage` | To save your language preferences locally |
| `tabs` | To update the extension badge (ON/OFF indicator) |
| `*.youtube.com` | To operate only on YouTube pages |
| `img.shields.io` | To display Chrome Web Store stats on welcome page |

### Does YuLaF use analytics or tracking?
**No.** We don't use Google Analytics, tracking pixels, or any analytics service. Zero tracking.

### What about children's privacy?
YuLaF doesn't collect any data from anyone, including children. The extension is safe for all ages.

### Can I trust YuLaF with my privacy?
Yes. We've made our privacy policy public and transparent. You can review it anytime at [PRIVACY.md](PRIVACY.md).
